simsun_perm_cyc_oexc_eexc

SimSun Permutation in cycles form, with o_exc, e_exc (back to Data page)

定義：

  • SimSun Permutation of [n]： A permutation $\rho \in \mathbb{S}_n$ is simsun if for all $k \leq n$, the subword of $\rho$ restricted to [k] does not have three consecutive decreasing elements.

  • o_exc($\rho$) = # $\{i \mid \rho(i): \mbox{ odd and } \rho(\rho(i)) > \rho(i) \}$

  • e_exc($\rho$) = # $\{i \mid \rho(i): \mbox{ even and } \rho(\rho(i)) > \rho(i) \}$
